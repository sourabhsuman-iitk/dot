About Us

Dot is a company that focuses on digitally enabling and empowering small and medium merchants in the country. We strive to create a seamless omnichannel experience for customers by integrating brands, merchants, and customers both digitally and physically. Our primary goal is to support and uplift small and medium-sized businesses by providing them with the necessary tools and resources to thrive in the digital era.

By partnering with Dot, merchants gain access to an extensive and comprehensive assortment of products, ensuring that they can offer a wide variety of options to their customers. We also aim to level the playing field for these merchants, allowing them to compete effectively with larger retail giants.

Through our platform, they can access best-in-class offers, enabling them to provide competitive pricing and deals to their customers. Additionally, we prioritize convenience, making it easier for merchants to streamline their operations and enhance their overall customer experience. Whether it's through digital solutions or physical integration, Dot is committed to empowering small and medium merchants to reach their full potential and succeed in the modern retail landscape.

Dot Partner Program: Empowering Merchants with Deep Discounts and More

Dot presents an exciting opportunity for merchants, commercial space owners, and opportunists to partner with us and unlock the potential of selling products both online and offline. With our omnichannel platform, you can start and grow your business while leveraging our cutting-edge technology, robust supply chain, and substantial buying power. But that's not all.

The Dot Partner Program offers a unique advantage that sets us apart from the competition: the ability to provide deep discounts to your customers, just like retail giants such as Dmart. Imagine the power to offer highly competitive prices that attract thrifty shoppers and position your business for success.

To become a Dot partner, you only need to have a shop space and a commitment to growing your business.

Here's how it works
Use your shop space to store inventory

Allow us to utilize your shop as a storage location for products.

Operate as a physical store and accept online orders

Continue running your shop as a normal physical store while also accepting online orders through our platform.

Delivery handled by our partners

The delivery of online orders will be managed by our reliable delivery partners

As a Dot partner

Your income will grow through the following means:
Retail margins

You will earn the same retail margins as before, similar to what other merchants earn

Credit on inventory

You will receive the entire inventory on credit until the products are sold to customers.

Deep discounts

You can provide deep discounts to customers, similar to what retail and e-commerce giants like Dmart offer, without reducing your margins.

Your role as a Dot partner involves:

Store and inventory management

You will be responsible for managing the store and inventory, ensuring everything is well-organized and stocked.

Handling physical customers

You will continue serving customers who visit your store in person.

Online order management

You need to accurately and promptly manage online orders before our delivery partners arrive at your store for pickup

Billing through DotPOS

Use our DotPOS system to handle billing and payment transactions for both offline and online orders.

Return order management

Collect return orders from our delivery partners in cases where customers wish to return products.
